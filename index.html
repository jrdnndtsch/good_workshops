About the Workshop

This Shopify app development workshop is targeted at developers who are looking to jump into app development for e-commerce projects. As part of this workshop, we will introduce you to a variety of resources to support your development with Shopify.

Using Ruby on Rails as a back-end framework, this workshop begins by building a base Shopify Private Application to integrate with one of your own development stores. You'll leave this workshop with a set of steps to start up any new app development project, with a greater understanding of how clients or your own shops could be served by a private app.
